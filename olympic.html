Volleyball at the Olympics

Volleyball was introduced at the 1964 Olympics in Tokyo/Japan. Nowadays the Olympic tournament has the following competition formula:

Qualification Round

  • Twelve teams participate in each event and the host nation is pre-qualified.
  • Five teams qualify as winners of continental qualification tournaments, and three teams qualify through the World Cup.
  • The three remaining berths are decided in world qualification tournaments.

Competition Format

  • At the first phase each team plays one match against all others Top four teams in each pool advance
  • At the second phase, usually called final round, teams play quarterfinals, semifinals and finals.
  • At the finals, winners of semifinals play for the gold, and losers for the bronze.
