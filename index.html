Welcome to the World of Volleyball

This site is divided in many sections. Here you can see some information about them.

History and Rules: This section shows main historical events of the sport, from its creation to present days. On the other hand, rules represent an important complement since within every change the sport has become more competitive.

Skills and Strategy: In this section, the basic skills to play Volleyball will be better analyzed. Serve, Pass, Set, Attack, Block and Dig. Concerning Strategy, topics like player positions and formations will be covered.

Beach Volley: this part of the site will talk a bit about this related game, which is played in dual.

Curiosities: some cool/fun/interesting facts about the world of Volleyball. Ex: how many jumps a Volleyball player executes during a match?

Competition: This sections is formed by two subsections:

  • Volleyball in Brazil: The objective here is to describe, above all after 1992, how the sport has developed in my client’s homeland.
  • Volleyball in the Olympic Games: complete report of all the champions throughout the history of the Olympic Games, and a top five ranking of the winning nations.
